About

I am a researcher working at the interface of environmental modelling, machine learning, hydrology, water quality, and river/coastal systems. My work focuses on using data-driven and process-informed methods to understand complex environmental behaviour across aquatic systems.

Research Interests

  • Machine learning for environmental and hydrological systems
  • Coastal water quality and bacterial contamination analysis
  • River morphodynamics and meandering river modelling
  • Physics-informed neural networks for environmental processes
  • Dam regulation, inundation dynamics, and fisheries response
  • Groundwater and river salinity / TDS prediction

Selected Projects

  • Coastal bacterial concentration mapping: deep-learning-based mapping of bacterial concentration patterns in coastal environments.
  • Meandering river morphodynamics: hybrid physics-informed neural network modelling of river planform evolution.
  • Tide-aware E. coli analysis: conditional lag-based analysis of stream and tidal influence on coastal bacterial variability.
  • Dam-aware CPUE analysis: investigation of flow regulation, inundation dynamics, and fisheries response.
  • TDS prediction: machine-learning models for salinity and total dissolved solids prediction in river and aquifer systems.
